How to Increase Your Technology RPM

You can't control the global average, but you can control these 5 factors:

  1. Target US traffic: US visitors pay {{US_MULTIPLIER}}x more than the global average. Write for US audiences. Use US spelling, US examples, US references.
  2. Write 2,000+ word articles: Dwell time matters. Technology audiences engage more with long-form content, which increases ad impressions and RPM.
  3. Place ads "in-content": In-content ads have 3-5x higher CTR than sidebar ads. Place ads between paragraphs, not just in the sidebar.
  4. Use structured formats: Lists, how-tos, and comparison tables keep users on the page longer.
  5. Update your content every 6 months: Stale content drops RPM by 20-30%. A simple refresh can restore that lost revenue.

The 80/20 rule: Spend 80% of your effort on traffic geography (US/UK/CA) and 20% on content depth. That's how top publishers hit 2-3x the average RPM.
