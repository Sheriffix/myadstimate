What is RPM? RPM stands for Revenue Per Mille (revenue per 1,000 pageviews). It's the standard metric used by AdSense publishers to measure earnings performance. A higher RPM means more revenue per visitor.

The data in these guides is based on 2026 industry benchmarks, combining country-specific base RPMs with niche-specific multipliers. The result is a realistic estimate of what publishers can expect to earn from each combination.

What is a niche-country RPM guide?

It's a detailed analysis of AdSense earning potential for a specific content niche (like Finance or Insurance) targeting traffic from a specific country (like the US or Iceland). Each guide breaks down the expected RPM, CPC, and optimization strategies.

How is Expected RPM calculated?

Expected RPM = Country Base RPM × Niche Multiplier. The country base RPM reflects advertiser demand in that market, while the niche multiplier reflects how much advertisers are willing to pay for that content vertical.
