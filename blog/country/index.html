This comprehensive directory shows base RPM rates for 80+ countries, organized by tier classification. These rates represent the baseline earnings before niche multipliers are applied. Combine these geographic rates with your niche's multiplier to calculate your true earning potential.

Understanding Geographic Impact

These base RPM rates represent the starting point for your earnings potential. Your actual revenue will be the result of multiplying these geographic rates by your niche's multiplier. For example, a Finance website targeting United States traffic would calculate: $20 (US base RPM) × 2.5 (Finance multiplier) = $50 expected RPM.
