Our Editorial & Data Standards

To maintain our position as a trusted authority in the monetization space, we adhere to a strict framework for every benchmark and guide we publish.

1. Data Integrity & Verification

Our Revenue Matrix is not a collection of static historical figures. We utilize a proprietary logic that weighs global CPC trends, regional inflation markers, and industry-specific demand shifts. Each of our 2,520 unique benchmarks is cross-referenced against current market volatility to ensure our estimates remain within a high-confidence interval.

2. Human-Centric Analysis

We reject the "low-value content" model. While we use advanced logic to process large datasets, every troubleshooting guide and strategic article on this site is authored by a subject matter expert. Our insights come from hands-on experience in server-side optimization, SEO architecture, and AdSense account management.

3. Independence & Transparency

Adstimate is a fully independent platform. We do not accept payment to "rank" specific niches higher or to favor one ad network over another. Our comparisons are based strictly on technical performance metrics and user-centric revenue data.
